Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

Our Core Principle: ThreadClear is designed with privacy-first architecture. We do not store, retain, or share your conversation data. All analysis is performed ephemerally.

1. Information We Process

When you use the ThreadClear browser extension, we process:

  • Conversation text: Text you select or paste into the extension for analysis
  • Draft messages: Optional draft replies you submit for feedback

This data is sent to our secure API for analysis and is not stored after processing is complete.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store your conversation content
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not access any content unless you explicitly select it or paste it

3. How We Process Data

When you click "Analyze":

  1. Your selected/pasted text is sent via encrypted HTTPS to our API
  2. Our AI analyzes the conversation for insights (unanswered questions, tension points, etc.)
  3. Results are returned to your browser
  4. The original text is immediately discarded from our servers

We call this ephemeral processing â€“ your data exists only for the duration of the analysis request.

4. Data Storage

The extension may store locally on your device:

  • Your preferred settings (e.g., source type selection)

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by removing the extension.

5. Third-Party Services

Our analysis uses AI services (such as Anthropic Claude) to process conversations. These services:

  • Receive only the text you submit for analysis
  • Do not retain your data after processing
  • Are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements

6. Permissions Explained

The extension requests these permissions:

  • activeTab: To read text you've selected on the current page (only when you click the extension)
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • scripting: To capture selected text from web pages

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function.

7. Data Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Our API is hosted on Microsoft Azure with enterprise-grade security
  • No conversation data is logged or stored on our servers

8. Children's Privacy

ThreadClear is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly process data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't store your data, there is nothing to delete or export. If you have concerns about privacy, simply stop using the extension â€“ no residual data will remain on our servers.
