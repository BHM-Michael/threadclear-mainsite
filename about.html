About ThreadClear

Building conversation intelligence for teams that can't afford to miss anything.

Our Mission

No conversation should fall through the cracks

Every day, critical questions go unanswered. Tension builds unnoticed. Teams misalign without realizing it. These small gaps compound into missed deadlines, damaged relationships, and lost opportunities.

ThreadClear exists to surface what matters most in your conversations—unanswered questions, emerging tensions, and potential misalignments—so you can address them before they become problems.

Why Privacy-First

Some teams can't use traditional SaaS

Legal teams handling privileged communications. Healthcare providers discussing patient care. Financial advisors managing sensitive client relationships. Defense contractors working on classified projects.

These organizations need conversation intelligence, but they can't send their data to cloud services. They need solutions that process ephemerally, deploy on-premises, and work in air-gapped environments.

That's exactly what ThreadClear provides. We built privacy into our architecture from day one—not as an afterthought, but as our core differentiator. Your conversations are processed in memory and never retained. For maximum security, deploy ThreadClear on your own infrastructure.

Our Approach

Intelligence without compromise

ThreadClear uses a hybrid architecture that combines pattern recognition with AI analysis. Most conversations are processed using sophisticated regex patterns—fast, deterministic, and entirely local. AI is called only when needed for nuanced analysis, and even then, we use ephemeral processing that retains nothing.

The result: enterprise-grade conversation intelligence with the privacy guarantees that regulated industries require.

Hybrid Processing

80-90% of analysis done via local patterns. AI called only when needed.

Zero Retention

Conversations processed in memory. Nothing stored after analysis.

Self-Hosted Option

Deploy on your own infrastructure for maximum control.

The Company

Built by BHM-IT

ThreadClear is developed by BHM-IT, LLC, a digital services company focused on building privacy-first solutions for regulated industries. We understand the unique challenges that legal, healthcare, finance, and defense organizations face when adopting new technology.

Our goal isn't to compete with big tech conversation tools. It's to serve the customers they can't—organizations that require the highest levels of data privacy and security.
