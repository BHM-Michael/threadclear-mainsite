Introduction

ThreadClear ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our conversation analysis service.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Organization name (if applicable)

Conversation Data

When you use ThreadClear to analyze conversations, the text you submit is processed to provide analysis results. We do not store your conversation content. All conversation data is processed ephemerally and discarded immediately after analysis is complete.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data including:

  • Number of analyses performed
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error logs for troubleshooting

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Process your conversation analyses
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Send important service updates
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

Data Retention

Conversation Content: Not retained. Processed ephemerally and immediately discarded.

Account Data: Retained while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Usage Analytics: Retained in anonymized form for up to 12 months.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Microsoft Azure: Cloud hosting and infrastructure
  • AI Providers: For conversation analysis (data is not retained by these providers)
  • Stripe: Payment processing (we do not store credit card information)

Integrations

When you connect ThreadClear to Slack, Microsoft Teams, or Outlook:

  • We only access data you explicitly submit for analysis
  • We do not read or store your messages without your action
  • Integration tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Children's Privacy

ThreadClear is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
