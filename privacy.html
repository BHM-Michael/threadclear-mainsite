Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Introduction

ThreadClear ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by BHM-IT, LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our conversation intelligence platform at threadclear.com and app.threadclear.com (the "Service").

Our Service is built on a fundamental principle: your conversation data is never retained. We process conversations ephemerally—in memory only—and discard all content immediately after analysis.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Company name and industry (optional)
  • Password (stored securely hashed)

Conversation Data

We do not retain conversation content. When you submit conversations for analysis:

  • Content is processed entirely in memory
  • Analysis results are generated and returned to you
  • All conversation content is immediately discarded
  • No conversation data is written to disk, logged, or stored

Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage statistics to improve our Service:

  • Number of analyses performed (not content)
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Performance metrics

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process your conversation analyses
  • Send you important service updates
  • Improve our algorithms and features
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Retention

Conversation Data: Zero retention. All conversation content is processed in memory and immediately discarded. We do not store, log, or retain any conversation text, images, or audio.

Account Data: Retained while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Usage Data: Anonymized analytics are retained for up to 24 months.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help operate our Service (e.g., cloud hosting), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

AI Processing

ThreadClear uses artificial intelligence to analyze conversations. When AI processing is required:

  • Data is transmitted securely using TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Processing is ephemeral—no data is retained by AI providers
  • We use multiple AI providers; you may choose your preferred provider in settings
  • Self-hosted deployments can use local AI models with no external transmission

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure

For detailed security information, see our Security page.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your account data
  • Correction: Update your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your account data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@threadclear.com.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Ensure security

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

Children's Privacy

ThreadClear is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

International Transfers

Our Service is hosted in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. Self-hosted deployments can maintain all data within your preferred jurisdiction.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by email or through the Service. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
